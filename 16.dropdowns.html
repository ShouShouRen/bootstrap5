Example Explained The .collapse class indicates a collapsible element (a div in our example); this is the content that will be shown or hidden with a click of a button. To control (show/hide) the collapsible content, add the data-bs-toggle="collapse" attribute to an a or a button element. Then add the data-bs-target="#id" attribute to connect the button with the collapsible content (div id="demo").
